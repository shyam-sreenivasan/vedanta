Who are we?

Vedanta Vidya Peetam is a not-for-profit institution with a goal of delivering hindu religious education and services. An abundance of knowledge is buried in hindu scriptures in the form of slokas and stories. Vedanta Vidya Peetam strives to bring these treasure to the children by way of conducting weekly classes. Vedanta vidya Peetam is also involved in contributing its might towards social causes by way of providing donations for orphanages, homeless shelters, public educational institutions, various hunger programs.

What we provide?

We provide services which includes slokha classes, moral stories for children, carnatic music , and priest pooja. For more information, check out the Services section.
